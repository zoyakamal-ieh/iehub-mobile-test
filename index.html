Welcome to the
IE Hub

Being part of an ISoc is the hardest work on campus. We help you lead with Barakah, not stress.

For too long, decades of FOSIS knowledge and institutional memory have been hidden away in private drives and scattered handovers. Our team has come together to change that by curating the very best resources, frameworks, and advice into one central space.

We want to ensure that no student has to reinvent the wheel. By sharing these essentials, we are stopping the duplication of work and creating a continuous stream of Sadaqqah Jariya for everyone involved in building the future of the Muslim student experience.
